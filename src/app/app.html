Filter Tips

  • Use comma-separated values for multi-token text search in OR/AND modes.
  • Switch to Regex mode for advanced matching patterns.
  • Global scope can target either all columns or visible columns.
  • Global highlights are green; column-specific highlights are blue.
  • Click chips to remove active filters quickly.
